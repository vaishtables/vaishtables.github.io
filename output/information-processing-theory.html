Term

information processing theory
a perspective that figures human cognition as analogous to computational analysis of data, consisting of sensory input, connections, memory storage, and ouput
emphasizes processes of thought in contrast to the Piagetian focus on responses ^[1]

[^1]: but Piaget also offered a theory that is considered to push back against stimulus-response views of cognition and moreover, also referenced cybernetics as an analogy for reinforcement learning "Cognitive Development in Children"
